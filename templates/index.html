Automated Timetable

Intelligently generate timetables for classes based on your schedule requirements and room availability. Customize period names, sizes and more.

Attendance Tracking

Record daily student attendance digitally via CSV import or manual entry for each class. Timestamps provide verification.

Data Analytics

Visualize attendance statistics through interactive charts by date range, student, class or school-wide. Gain insights into absenteeism.

Access Controls

Manage role-based access for teachers, administrators, students. Restrict permissions to guarantee privacy and compliance.

Custom Settings

Administrators can configure absence thresholds, class names, hours per day and other rules. Fine-tune to your policies.
